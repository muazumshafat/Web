Who We Are / Our Mission

KYN is a non-profit organization dedicated to empowering Kashmiri youth by providing resources and opportunities in education, community engagement, mental wellness, and leadership.

We design programs to be inclusive, accessible, and impactful — building a brighter future for the next generation of leaders.
